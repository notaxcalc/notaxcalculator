# Changelog

All formula and methodology changes are documented here.

## [v1.1] — July 2025

### Added
- Tips deduction calculation as a separate, additive deduction
- Tips deduction cap: $12,500 (single) / $25,000 (MFJ)
- Tips and overtime savings now displayed independently in output

### Changed
- Total savings output now reflects combined OT + tips deduction

---

## [v1.0] — July 2025

### Added
- Initial release
- OBBBA overtime premium deduction formula
- Single and Married Filing Jointly filing statuses
- Phase-out reduction: $100 per $1,000 of MAGI above threshold
- Deduction cap: $12,500 (single) / $25,000 (MFJ)
- 2025 federal income tax bracket lookup
- Standard deduction applied before bracket lookup
